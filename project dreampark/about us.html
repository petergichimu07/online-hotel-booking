About Dreampark Retreat Center

Dreampark Retreat Center is a Resort located on the entry of Dream National Park. We have been offering retreat and restourant services since 1969. The park was originally started to harbour rare wild animals. As a result, we have the rarest animals, some of which can never he found in any other parks around the country or anywhere in Africa. We are located 90 kilometres from Nairobi CBD, on Mombasa Road.
